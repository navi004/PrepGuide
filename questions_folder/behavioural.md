## Behavioral Questions

1. Tell me about a time you were working on a team project and had a fundamental disagreement with a teammate about a technical approach. How did you handle the situation, and what was the outcome?
2. Describe the most complex technical bug or challenge you've encountered in one of your projects. Walk me through your step-by-step process for diagnosing the problem and what you learned from the experience.
3. As a member of the Microsoft Innovation Club, can you give an example of a time you took the initiative to lead a project or organize an activity that was not explicitly assigned to you? What was the result of your initiative?
4. Imagine you need to explain your Quantum CNN project to a non-technical project manager at Deloitte. How would you describe the project's purpose, approach, and business value in simple, understandable terms?
5. The tech landscape, especially in AI, changes rapidly. How do you stay current with new tools and techniques? Describe a new technology or library you've learned in the past six months and how you applied it to a project.
